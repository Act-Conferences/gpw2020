Call for Presentations

The German Perl and Software Engineering Workshop takes place from 4th to 6th March 2020 in Erlangen, Germany.

We are looking for your contribution in the form a talk (20 minutes or 40 minutes), a lighting talk (5 minutes) or a workshop (2-4 hours). Please submit your proposals using this online form.

In addition to traditional Perl (5 or 6) topics, we are looking for software engineering and software craftsmanship content.

Some examples of what we are looking for:

  • Modern Perl 5 topics, such as
    • Moose & co tutorials
    • Asynchronous programming
    • REST API construction
  • Perl 6-related talks and workshops
  • Software Architecture
  • Application and network security
  • Domain-Driven Design
  • Software Craftsmanship
  • Continuous Integration and Delivery (CI/CD)
  • Experiences with large software systems

Talks in English are preferred over German talks. For German talks, we ask the speakers to at least have slides in English.

We welcome talk submissions from a diverse background of speakers, and from minorities.

Please submit your talks until Sunday, November 24th, 2019.
